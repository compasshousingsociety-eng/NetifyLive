💡 Resident Safety Tips

Simple practices that make a big difference in personal safety

1

Always Lock Your Room

Even when stepping out briefly, ensure your room door is securely locked. Use the deadbolt at night.

2

Know Emergency Exits

Familiarize yourself with fire exits, assembly points, and emergency equipment locations.

3

Share Your Schedule

Inform housemates or security about late-night arrivals or extended absences.

4

Verify Visitors

Always check ID and register visitors at the front desk. Never allow unverified entries.

5

Keep Emergency Contacts Handy

Save all emergency numbers on speed dial and keep a physical copy in your room.

6

Report Suspicious Activity

Immediately report any suspicious behavior to security or management.
