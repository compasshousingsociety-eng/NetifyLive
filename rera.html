📜 Your Rights Under RERA (PG Residents)

As a resident in a RERA-registered PG or hostel, you are entitled to transparency, timely possession, and quality living standards.

Standardized Rental Agreement

RERA mandates registered model agreements with clear terms on deposit, rent, maintenance, and notice period for all PG accommodations.

Timely Possession & Services

Delayed move-in? RERA protects your rights. All Netify properties adhere to committed possession dates and service timelines.

Defect Liability & Maintenance

Structural defects or amenity issues must be rectified within 30 days. RERA ensures prompt maintenance response in our hostels.

Grievance Redressal Forum

RERA provides an appellate authority. Residents can escalate unresolved PG/hostel complaints to the Real Estate Regulatory Authority.

RERA Compliance Documents

Access registration certificates, project details, and sanctioned plans for all Netify PG properties.
