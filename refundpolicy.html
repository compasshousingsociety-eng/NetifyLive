Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear & Transparent Refund Terms

At Netify Living, we believe in complete transparency. Here's everything you need to know about our cancellation and refund policies.

Last Updated: March 15, 2025 | Version 2.0

1. Introduction

This Refund Policy ("Policy") outlines the terms and conditions regarding cancellations, refunds, and security deposit returns for bookings and services provided by Netify Living. We strive to maintain fairness and clarity in all our financial transactions.

By making a booking with Netify Living, you agree to abide by the terms of this Policy. We recommend reading this document carefully before proceeding with any payment.

2. Cancellation Timeline & Refunds

The refund amount depends on the timing of your cancellation relative to your move-in date. Please refer to the table below:

Cancellation Period Refund Percentage Additional Notes
30+ days before move-in 100% Refund Full refund minus any processing fees (₹500)
15-29 days before move-in 75% Refund 25% cancellation fee applies
7-14 days before move-in 50% Refund 50% cancellation fee applies
1-6 days before move-in 25% Refund 75% cancellation fee applies
On or after move-in date 0% Refund No refund for cancellation after move-in

Note: All refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation confirmation and will be credited to the original payment method.

3. Security Deposit Refunds

Security deposits are collected at the time of booking and are refundable at the end of your stay, subject to the following conditions:

  • Full Refund: If the room is vacated in good condition, no damages, and all dues cleared.
  • Partial Refund: Deductions may apply for damages, unpaid bills, or cleaning charges beyond normal wear and tear.
  • Processing Time: Security deposit refunds are processed within 15-20 business days after check-out.
  • Deductions: Itemized list of deductions (if any) will be shared with the resident.

Full Deposit Refund

No damages, no pending dues, proper check-out process completed

Partial Deductions

Minor damages, cleaning charges, or utility bills pending

No Refund

Major damages, early termination without notice, or breach of contract

5. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Early Termination: Cancellation after the lock-in period without proper notice (30 days).
  • Violation of Policies: Eviction due to violation of house rules or illegal activities.
  • No-Show: Failure to check-in on the scheduled date without prior intimation.
  • Special Discounts: Bookings made under promotional offers or non-refundable plans.
  • Force Majeure: Natural disasters, government orders, or unforeseen circumstances beyond our control.

6. Special Circumstances

We understand that life can be unpredictable. In special cases, we may consider exceptions:

  • Medical Emergencies: Full or partial refund may be provided with valid medical documentation.
  • Job Relocation: Proof of relocation may qualify for reduced cancellation fees.
  • Family Emergencies: Each case reviewed individually with appropriate documentation.

For special circumstances, please email us at refunds@netifyliving.com with supporting documents.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once approved, refunds are processed within 7-10 business days. The actual time to reflect in your account depends on your bank/payment provider (usually 3-5 additional days).

Can I transfer my booking to someone else instead of canceling?

Yes, booking transfers are allowed once per booking with a nominal transfer fee of ₹1,000. The new occupant must meet all eligibility criteria and complete verification.

What happens to my refund if I paid via EMI or credit card?

Refunds are processed to the original payment method. For EMI transactions, the refund will be credited to your card, and the EMI schedule will be adjusted by your bank.

Will I get my security deposit back if I extend my stay?

Your security deposit will remain with us until the final check-out. If you extend your stay, the deposit will be adjusted toward the new booking period.

What if Netify Living cancels my booking?

In the rare event we need to cancel your booking, you'll receive a 100% full refund along with assistance in finding alternative accommodation.
