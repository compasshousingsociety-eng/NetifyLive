Redefining Coliving
for Modern India

We're on a mission to transform the PG and hostel experience by creating vibrant, secure, and community-driven living spaces where students and professionals can thrive.

Our Mission

To provide premium, hassle-free coliving experiences that combine comfort, community, and convenience, enabling residents to focus on what matters most - their growth and success.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and beloved coliving brand, setting new standards in organized living with 500+ properties across 25+ cities by 2030.

From a Dream to
India's Largest Coliving Network

Founded in 2017, Netify Living started with a simple observation: students and working professionals moving to new cities deserved better than cramped, poorly-managed PGs. We envisioned a world where coliving meant more than just a bed - it meant a home, a community, and a launchpad for dreams.

Today, we've grown into India's fastest-growing managed accommodation network, serving over 50,000+ residents across 15+ cities. Every Netify Living property is designed to foster connections, ensure safety, and provide the amenities that modern lifestyles demand.
