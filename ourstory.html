Our Story: Redefining PG & Coliving in India

What began as a simple idea — turning impersonal hostels into vibrant, supportive communities — is now a movement transforming how students and professionals live across the country.

From a single PG to a network of homes

Netify Living was born in 2020 when our founders — former hostel residents themselves — realized that traditional PGs lacked warmth, community, and flexibility. We set out to build spaces where ambition meets comfort. Today, we operate premium PG and coliving spaces across Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Chennai, with a commitment to transparency, safety, and belonging.

Unlike conventional PG hostels, we curate every detail: chef-cooked meals, high-speed WiFi, dedicated workspaces, and community managers who ensure you never feel alone. Over 5,000+ residents have called Netify their home away from home.

Our mission is simple: make quality coliving accessible, joyful, and hassle-free for every young Indian. We don't just offer a bed; we offer a lifestyle built on trust, inclusivity, and growth.

What Makes Our PG Hostels Different

Values that guide every Netify PG residence

Safety First

Biometric access, CCTV, female-friendly floors, and trained wardens ensure peace of mind for every resident in our PG hostels.

Community Spirit

Regular movie nights, skill-sharing sessions, and celebrations turn neighbours into friends — the heart of modern coliving.

Hassle-Free Living

All-inclusive rents: meals, WiFi, laundry, maintenance. No hidden charges, just pure convenience in every PG stay.

Sustainable Stays

Eco-friendly practices, waste segregation, and energy-efficient buildings — because home should also care for the planet.

The Future of Coliving & PG Hostels

Netify Living is on a mission to become India's most loved PG and coliving ecosystem. We're expanding to 15+ cities by 2028, introducing flexible co-living memberships, and launching "Netify Young Professional" programs that connect residents with mentorship and career opportunities.

Every new PG property we design includes co-working zones, fitness corners, and sustainable infrastructure. We're reimagining hostels as spaces where creativity, comfort, and community thrive together. Join us as we continue to rewrite the rules of shared living.
