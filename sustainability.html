Sustainable Living at Netify

Our PG hostels are designed to inspire eco-conscious habits among residents. From energy-efficient lighting to smart building management systems, every detail matters.

  • LED lighting with motion sensors in common areas
  • Smart HVAC systems for optimal energy use
  • Biodegradable cleaning products across all properties
  • Digital-first operations reducing paper waste by 95%
  • Electric vehicle charging stations at select locations
